Stir fry Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pepper
  • Tomato
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Turkey
  • Groundnut oil

Steps

  • Boil 2 litres of water
  • Pour the spaghetti into the boiling water and leave to cook for about 6-7 minutes
  • Once the spaghetti has been cooked for about 6-7 minutes pour thr spaghetti into a seive
  • Make a sauce using your tomato and pepper
  • Add all your seasoning
  • Once the sauce is done pour your cooked spaghetti
  • Mix thouroughly until it is evenly mixed
  • Add your diced cabbage
  • Cover and leave it to steam for about 5 minutes

Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Pepper
  • Tomato Paste
  • Bay leaf
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Turkey
  • Groundnut oil

Steps

  • Parboil the rice
  • Blend your pepper and tomato
  • In a medium size pot, heat the oil and add onions. Leave it to fry till it turns golden brown
  • Add the blended pepper and tomato paste
  • After about 15-20 minutes add your seasonings
  • Add the parboiled rice to the sauce and mix it evenly
  • Add the bay leaves and cover the pot. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for about 15-30 minutes
  • Once the liquid is dried up, turn off the heat and your jollof rice is ready

Bread and egg

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Spring Onion
  • Pepper
  • Groundnut Oil

Steps

  • Dice your pepper, spring onion and onion
  • Mix diced ingredients with the egg
  • Place a non stick frying pan on fire
  • Pour groundnut oil into the frying pan
  • Fry the Egg
  • Slice open the bread into two and place the egg in it
  • Fry both side of the bread with the egg in it
